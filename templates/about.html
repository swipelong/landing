Swipelong was created to enable millennials to have an easy barrier of entry to learning the basics of investing
It was on cold January day in New York City when SwipeLong was born. Sitting at a table in Tribeca during a snowstorm two Bitcoin millionares were discussing the current climate of the stock makret and cryptocurrency. What they had seen over the past few years was explosive growth in the market, innovation and a US Economy that was seemingly on the rise. What they dhad heard from their
fellow Millenials was an unhappiness with the process of learning the basics of investing. At the end of the day what these two realized the issue was with engagement and how simple informaiton could initially be delivered. It was that day when they made the first mock of up what is now SwipeLong. The easiest way to swipe, match chat, and learn the basics of investing.
